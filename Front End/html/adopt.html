Mapping Frictions is a website dedicated to offering new multimedia content and celebrating untold stories from Western Sydney. By providing a fresh, bold perspective on Western Sydney in a new multimedia context, the project is an innovative approach to community building and digital storytelling in Australia. We are looking for new contributors and content in a range of forms, including fiction, articles, and photography.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

We are interested in local interest stories that tap into wider issues. We want content that reflects the vibrancy, complexity, and diversity of Sydney’s western suburbs.

ARTICLES AND STORIES

  • Articles 500-1000 words.
  • Stories 800 words max.
  • Word document preferable. Simple 12 point font.
  • Suggest an engaging title.
  • References. Link to any articles, claims, facts or quotes that you refer to. Use footnotes when hyperlink not available. Paraphrasing preferred unless it’s an indispensable quote.
  • Use media like YouTube or embedded audio if it helps you tell your story. Paste a direct link into the body of the text exactly where you would like us to embed it.

OTHER MEDIUMS

  • Do you have multimedia or illustration ideas? Please send us a sample of your work and get in contact with us to discuss.
